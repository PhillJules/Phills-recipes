Coconut Jerk Chicken

Description

Prepare your taste buds for a flavorful journey with our Coconut Jerk Chicken a mouthwatering fusion of Caribbean-inspired spices and creamy coconut. This dish combines the heat of jerk seasoning with the soothing richness of coconut, resulting in a tantalizing experience that's impossible to resist. Served over a bed of steaming brown rice, it's a harmonious blend of spice and comfort.

  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 2 teaspoons dry Caribbean jerk seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons chicken bouillon granules
  • 1 yellow onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh minced garlic
  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 cup peeled, seeded, and diced butternut squash
  • 8 ounces button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 bunch green onions, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
