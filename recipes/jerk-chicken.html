Coconut Jerk Chicken

Description

Prepare your taste buds for a flavorful journey with our Coconut Jerk Chicken a mouthwatering fusion of Caribbean-inspired spices and creamy coconut. This dish combines the heat of jerk seasoning with the soothing richness of coconut, resulting in a tantalizing experience that's impossible to resist. Served over a bed of steaming brown rice, it's a harmonious blend of spice and comfort.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 2 teaspoons dry Caribbean jerk seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons chicken bouillon granules
  • 1 yellow onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh minced garlic
  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1 cup peeled, seeded, and diced butternut squash
  • 8 ounces button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 bunch green onions, cut into 1/4-inch pieces
  • 1 (13.5 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes, chopped, juice reserved
  • Â½ cup tomato juice
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Stir in the curry powder, jerk seasoning, bouillon granules, onions, and garlic.
  3. Stir and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Reduce heat to medium, add the chicken, and continue to stir and cook 6 to 8 minutes more.
  5. Stir in the squash and mushrooms.
  6. Reserve 1 tablespoon green onions for garnish, and stir in the remaining amount.
  7. Continue to stir and cook 2 minutes more.
  8. Pour in the coconut milk, tomatoes, 1/2 cup reserved tomato juice, and sugar.
  9. Reduce heat to low, and simmer for 35 to 40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  10. Serve, using 1 tablespoon green onions for garnish.
  11. Enjoy!
