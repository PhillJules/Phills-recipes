My Favorite Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with alternating layers of pasta, cheese, sauce, and meat. It is a staple of Italian cuisine and one of the most popular dishes around the world.

There's nothing quite like a classic homemade lasagna, and this recipe delivers a hearty, flavorful, and comforting dish that's perfect for sharing with family and friends. Making this lasagna a day ahead and refrigerating it overnight allows the spices to meld and gives it exceptional flavor. Here's what you can expect from this delicious lasagna:

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • 2 ½ teaspoons salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 12 dry lasagna noodles
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
