One Skillet Burrito Recipe

Description:

The Easy One-Skillet Ground Beef Burrito is a delicious and convenient twist on traditional burritos that's perfect for busy weeknights or whenever you're craving a hearty, satisfying meal. This recipe simplifies the burrito-making process, allowing you to enjoy all the flavors of a classic burrito without the fuss of assembling individual wraps.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, partially drained
  • 1 cup salsa
  • â…” cup water
  • 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning
  • 4 (8 inch) flour tortillas, cut into strips
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) carton sour cream, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Remove ground beef to a paper towel-lined plate and drain and discard grease from the skillet.
  3. Return ground beef to the skillet.
  4. add black beans, salsa, water, and taco seasoning and Mix well and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat slightly and add Cheddar cheese and tortilla strips.
  6. Cover and simmer until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
  7. Top with sour cream.
  8. Enjoy!
